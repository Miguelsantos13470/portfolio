about the game

Elden Cats is an open-world action role-playing game developed by MeStudios and published by Rotten Potato. Released in 2022, it was created under the direction of Predo NoiaMan, with collaboration from writer "The Great Matheus, Creator of Worlds" in building the game's universe and mythology. Set in the Asian Lands, the player assumes the role of a "cat" seeking the power of the Elden Cat to become the new Lord of the Cats. The game stands out for its dark atmosphere, challenging combat, freedom of exploration, and fragmented narrative, characteristic of the developer. It is widely acclaimed for its artistic direction, world design, and gameplay depth.
